About this Webpage

This webpage is the home of my original interactive math visualizations, which cover topics from discrete math to number theory. These are written primarily in Javascript using the HTML5 Canvas, with the exception of those created using the Desmos graphing calculator. They are designed to be interactive, intuitive, and fun. They will be improved and updated frequently.

About the Author

My name is Carly Matson, and I received my Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Colorado at Boulder in May of 2020. In January 2021 I began learning Javascript and using it to create interactive math visualizations.
